Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Harmony ("Harmony", "we", "us", or "our") provides a neutral, educational interfaith companion application that offers daily verses, a convergence calendar, and an AI-powered oracle ("Sage").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically (On-Device Only)

  • App preferences and settings (e.g., notification time for Daily Verse).
  • Local usage data such as viewed verses, calendar interactions, and conversation history (stored only on your device).

Important: The vast majority of Harmony operates entirely on your device. No personal data leaves your phone unless you use the Sage AI feature.

Information Sent When Using Sage (AI Oracle)

When you send a message to Sage, your query is sent through our secure proxy to Anthropic to generate a response. We do not store your conversations long-term on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to deliver the Service, improve the app, enforce usage limits, and respond to support requests.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We only share information with Anthropic (for AI responses) and Apple (for Sign in with Apple and subscriptions). We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can permanently delete everything at any time via Settings → Delete everything.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You control notifications, data deletion, and Sage usage directly in the app. You can manage Sign in with Apple through your Apple ID settings.
