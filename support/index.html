02 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my data?

Open the app and go to Settings → Delete everything. This permanently removes all locally stored data, including your conversation history and preferences.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through the App Store. You can manage or cancel them anytime in your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Is my data private?

Yes. Harmony runs almost entirely on your device. The only exception is when you use Sage, our AI oracle, which sends your query through a secure proxy to generate a response. See our Privacy Policy for details.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

We'd love to hear about it. Email us at support@harmonydailywisdom.com with as much detail as you can share, including your device model and iOS version.
